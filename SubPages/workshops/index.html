Workshops

Workshops at E-Summit'21 aim to deliver numerous tips-and-tricks to the attendees in order to achieve various creative, innovative and feasible ideas from daily life problems. They broaden the understanding of attendees of diverse aspects of entrepreneurship starting from idea generation to idea commercialization. It also lays emphasis on how a strong networking in the society/industry can help one to rise to a sustainable business enterprise and help them market the product to a much wider audience. The workshops stand with great benefits to the attendees as they learn how to build a corporate identity and filter out ideas for high growth entrepreneurship via various interactive sessions in these workshops with many distinguished personalities.
