About E-SUMMIT


                E-SUMMIT is a three day entrepreneurship conclave, started as an initiative of E-Cell, IIT Bhubaneswar. It is an annual celebration of innovation, creativity and business awareness.


It serves as a platform for young entrepreneurs, innovators, and industry leaders to connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.


Our fest includes various engaging activities such as workshops, seminars, panel discussions, competitions, and, of course, the YESTA.


YESTA Overview


               YESTA (Young Entrepreneur Skill and Talent Assessment) is an annual comprehensive assessment, conducted for students from grades sixth to twelfth.


It aims to challenge participants‚Äô critical thinking abilities, problem solving skills and knowledge of basic science and mathematics.


There will be three different category of papers for grades 6th, 7th and 8th, grades 9th and 10th and grades 11th and 12th.


The results for the same will be announced in the first week of August through our official website and social media handles. Winners and top scorers will also be notified through e-mails.
