About us



E-Summit is a three day event, started as an initiative of the E-Cell of IIT Bhubaneswar. The Summit seeks to facilitate interaction among industrialists, funding agencies, seasoned and upcoming entrepreneurs, academia and students of the participating institutes. E-Summitâ€™22 will include a gamut of activities such as entrepreneurial guest lectures, workshops and panel discussions with experts, startup internship fair and investors drive. E-summit, as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar, has proved its worth in depleting the Chasm that exists between E-and management. It is the stage for future Industry leaders and Entrepreneurs to exercise their skills.


"Where Investors Meets Innovators"

Embracing Innovation :
Reshaping The Future

Innovation involves a new way of thinking, thereby helping students develop their creativity and problem solving skills. In order to be competitive in this world where the expiration date of business models is shortening, we at E-Summit believe that we need to provide the future leaders with the skills and experience that give them the courage to imagine, test and implement new ways of doing business, ultimately giving them the cutting edge. We aim to openly embrace innovation and inventive design to bring people together and grow.
