About us



E-Summit is a three day event, started as an initiative of the Entrepreneurship Cell of IIT Bhubaneswar. The Summit seeks to facilitate interaction amonag industrialists, funding agencies, seasoned and upcoming entrepreneurs, academia and students of the participating institutes.

E-Summit’21 will include a gamut of activities such as entrepreneurial guest lectures, workshops and panel discussions with experts, startup internship fair and investors drive. E-summit, as a culture of IIT Bhubaneswar, has proved its worth in depleting the Chasm that exists between Entrepreneurship and management. It is the stage for future Industry leaders and Entrepreneurs to exercise their skills.
